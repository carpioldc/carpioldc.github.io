WhatsApp chat generator

Generate a link that will redirect your friends (if you have them) to a WhatsApp conversation with the recipient, showing the text on their message box. You can chain messages clicking on the Add button. A link leading to the next message will be added to each message text. I know it's not clear but what the hell just try it.

The messages will be sent in order from top to down.
